Controls

"F" - fly down

"R" - fly down

"E" - rotate right around spawn point

"Q" - rotate left around spawn point

"A" - move left

"D" - move right

"W" - move forward

"S" - move backwards

"CTRL + R" - reload

IMPORTANT: you are rotating around the center of the map which is where you started,
that means you may get lost when you are rotating around, if that hapens reload to regain composure
